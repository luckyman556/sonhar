QODE Quick View for WooCommerce helps you boost conversions & sales by providing visitors with handy pop-up product previews on product list pages.

== Description ==

<h3>Boost Your Conversion Rates with QODE Quick View</h3>

Turn your product listing pages into eye-catching, <strong>full-blown single product presentations</strong>.

<strong>QODE Quick View for WooCommerce</strong> provides you with a simple, user-friendly way to display compact forms dedicated to showcasing your single products in great detail. Quick View forms are triggered with a <strong>single click</strong> right from the <strong>product list</strong>, without ever leaving the page.

<a href="https://qodeinteractive.com/qode-quick-view-for-woocommerce/" target="_blank">Check out QODE Quick View Demo</a>

<h3>Fully Optimized for Viewing on Mobile Devices Too</h3>

Thanks to its <strong>full mobile optimization</strong>, the QODE Quick View can be accessed via <strong>any mobile device</strong> without any compromise in terms of the visuals and features, expanding the reach of this supremely practical eCommerce element once reserved only for desktop users.

<h3>Key features:</h3>

- Display the Quick View button in loops, which can be optionally disabled for mobile devices
- Open-ended Quick View button stylization, custom button labels and adjustable close icons

<h3>Get Even More With Premium!</h3>

The Premium version of the <a href="https://qodeinteractive.com/products/plugins/qode-quick-view-for-woocommerce/" target="_blank">QODE Quick View for WooCommerce</a> plugin provides you with three attention-grabbing Quick View form appearance styles to choose from – standard <strong>Pop-up</strong>, <strong>Sidebar</strong> Quick View, and cascading <strong>Drop</strong> Quick View form.

The <strong>size</strong> and <strong>appearance</strong> of the form can be easily adjusted thanks to the extensive set of customization options, and it can feature <strong>image galleries</strong> with <strong>thumbnails</strong> or <strong>sliders</strong> and optional <strong>image zoom</strong> on hover.

On top of that, the customization of the <strong>Quick View button</strong> appearance in lists is completely open-ended, allowing you to <strong>compose button & icon styles</strong> of your own.

The <a href="https://qodeinteractive.com/products/plugins/qode-quick-view-for-woocommerce/" target="_blank">QODE Quick View for WooCommerce Premium</a> lets you include a number of <strong>additional features</strong> inside your Quick Forms, providing you with a neat way to present your shoppers with all the relevant info relating to a product in a straightforward manner.

You can choose additional product information & features you wish to display, including the product <strong>category</strong>, <strong>rating</strong>, <strong>price</strong>, <strong>meta info</strong>, <strong>description</strong>, product <strong>tabs</strong>, a fixed <strong>‘View Details’ button</strong>, instantly loading <strong>AJAX-powered adding to cart</strong>, additional information <strong>items</strong>, adjustable <strong>social share</strong> icons, as well as <strong>suggested products</strong>.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I install QODE Quick View for WooCommerce? =

There are two ways you can install QODE Quick View for WooCommerce plugin.

You can install the plugin from your WordPress dashboard:
1. Navigate to your WordPress Dashboard -> Plugins -> Add New
2. Search for QODE Quick View for WooCommerce plugin
3. Install and Activate QODE Quick View for WooCommerce from your Plugins page.

Or you can install it directly from WordPress.org
1. Locate and download QODE Quick View for WooCommerce on wordpress.org
2. Upload the qode-quick-view-for-woocommerce directory to your ‘/wp-content/plugins/’ directory using your preferred method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc…)
3. Install and Activate QODE Quick View for WooCommerce plugin from your Plugins page.
